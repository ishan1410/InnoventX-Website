About Us

InnoventX is a robotics-based EdTech company providing a platform designed to help students to learn and understand in a fun and practical way.

Most students, nowadays suffer due to a lack of creativity and practical knowledge in their early years of learnings. The current job market is dominated by STEM, demand for candidates with qualifications in technology, specifically in robotics has seen a nearly 200 per cent growth.

Students lose opportunities, the reason being, they are not able to develop themselves according to the technological developments happening in the market.

To overcome this obstacle, we aim to provide students with an online and offline based robotics platform that will help them develop technical as well as soft skills.
